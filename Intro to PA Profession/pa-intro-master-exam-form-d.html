Before you begin

This is a 60-question comprehensive master exam spanning all seven Intro to PA Profession lecture decks — History of the PA Profession, PA Organizations, Professional Practice, Health Care is Teamwork, PAs and Clinical Practice, Professionalism, and Difficult Conversations. This is Form D, one of five parallel forms (A–E) drawing a different 60-question set from the same shared item pool, so your questions may not match a classmate's.

You'll answer one question at a time. After each answer you'll see whether you were right, an explanation, and why the other choices don't fit. A running score and progress bar track you along the way. At the end you get a full breakdown by topic and a review of everything you missed.

  • 1Trace the profession's history, organizational structure (ARC-PA, PAEA, NCCPA, AAPA and affiliates), and the certification/licensure/reimbursement framework that governs PA practice.
  • 2Explain the PA competency domains, Optimal Team Practice, and the regulatory/legal landmarks (EMTALA, malpractice policy types, credentialing vs. privileging) that shape professional practice.
  • 3Describe interprofessional teamwork, the roles of other health care providers, and the professionalism and academic-integrity standards expected of PA students and clinicians.
