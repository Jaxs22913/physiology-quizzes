Before you begin

This is a genuine comprehensive Master Exam spanning all seven Intro to PA Profession lecture decks: History of the PA Profession, PA Organizations, Professional Practice, Health Care is Teamwork, PAs and Clinical Practice, Professionalism, and Difficult Conversations. Form C is one of five parallel forms (A–E) of this exam, each drawing a different 60-question set from the same shared question pool.

You'll answer one question at a time. After each answer you'll see whether you were right, an explanation, and why the other choices don't fit. A running score and progress bar track you along the way. At the end you get a full breakdown by topic and a review of everything you missed.

  • 1Trace the history, key milestones, and governing organizations (ARC-PA, PAEA, NCCPA, AAPA, SAAAPA, PAF, FAPA, PAHx) that shaped the PA profession.
  • 2Describe the certification, licensure, credentialing, reimbursement, and legal/regulatory framework governing PA practice, including Florida-specific rules.
  • 3Explain how PAs function within interprofessional teams and clinical practice settings, and apply professionalism and difficult-conversation communication frameworks.
