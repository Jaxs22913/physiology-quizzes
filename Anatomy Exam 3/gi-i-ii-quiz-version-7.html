Before you begin

This is a fourth, independent set of questions covering the same Gastrointestinal Tract I & II lecture material — a fresh 30-item pass through the abdominal regions, peritoneum, general GI structure, stomach, spleen, small & large intestine, pancreas, liver, hepatic portal system, gallbladder, abdominal arterial supply, IVC, and abdominal lymphatics, for additional practice beyond the earlier quizzes.
