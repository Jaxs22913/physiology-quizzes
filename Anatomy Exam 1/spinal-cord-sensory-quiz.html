This exam covers the Spinal Cord & Sensory Receptors lecture, spanning the gross/sectional anatomy of the spinal cord, its major ascending and descending tracts, and the anatomical classification of sensory receptors (including olfaction and gustation). Every item is drawn only from that material and is built around the lecture's instructional objectives.
