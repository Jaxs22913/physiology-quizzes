## The Long Survey — Wren Calder, the Surveyor (post-apocalyptic)
Cross one ruined region on foot, top to bottom (ridge → drowned undercity), clearing
9 districts — one per PD Exam 2 topic, head-to-toe order. Content-themed boss names
(the Warden of the Rise, the Bile Merchant, the Sluicewarden...) but zero medical
claims in the prose. 100-question bank (BANK_AUDIT.md), scene map (WORLD.md).
