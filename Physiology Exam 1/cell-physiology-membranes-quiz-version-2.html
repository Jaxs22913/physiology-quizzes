Before you begin

This exam covers Unit 1: Cell Physiology and Cell Membrane, spanning homeostasis and feedback control, cell organelle structure/function, genetic control of cell function and reproduction, and the mechanisms of membrane transport (diffusion, active transport, osmosis, and tonicity). Every item is drawn only from that lecture and is built around its instructional objectives.

You'll answer one question at a time. After each answer you'll see whether you were right, an explanation, and why the other choices don't fit. A running score and progress bar track you along the way. At the end you get a full breakdown by topic and a review of everything you missed.

  • 2Explain endocytosis, exocytosis, apoptosis, and mitosis, and describe the functions of the different types of genetic material.
  • 3Compare and contrast the transport methods into and out of the cell, and describe the structure and function of cellular membranes.
